Dichotomy Powder Indexing v1.0

Finds potential unit cell parameters from a list of observed diffraction peaks using a systematic search method.

Supported File Formats:

Generic 2-column (2θ intensity), .xrdml, .brml, .ras, .uxd, .udf, GSAS .esd/.xra

Chart Interaction:

Zoom: Use the mouse wheel over an axis.

Pan: Click and drag the chart to pan.

Reset View: Right-click on the chart.

Add Peak: Hold Ctrl and click on the chart to manually add a peak position.
