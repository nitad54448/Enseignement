Le Bail Fitting

Refine lattice parameters and profile shape from powder diffraction data. This tool implements GSAS profile functions #2, #3, and #4. The method extracts integrated intensities based on calculated peak positions, then refines parameters to minimize the difference between observed and calculated profiles.

Based on A. Le Bail et al., J. Appl. Cryst. 21, 249-254 (1988).

Click and drag the plot, use the mouse wheel to zoom (zoom is axis-sensitive), or right-click to reset view.
